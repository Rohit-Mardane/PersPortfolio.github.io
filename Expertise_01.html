About Me

I'm an Research/Technical Writer

With a Master’s in Computer Aided Design and Computer Aided Manufacturing (CAD/CAM) and a Bachelor’s in Mechanical Engineering, and I have contributed to projects involving product lifecycle documentation, intelligent UX workflows, and robotic systems integration. My work ranges from product 3D modelling to authoring REST APIs documentation; designing user manuals to developing content management and processiong utilities using python and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) models.

Beyond tools and deliverables, I am driven by the idea that great technologies deserve great communication — especially in robotics, where human-machine interaction must be intuitive and safe. My passion lies at the intersection of robotics, AI-driven assistive technologies, and human-centered design. Currently based in Munich, I am exploring research opportunities, collaborations, and R&D roles where I can contribute to meaningful innovations in robotics, particularly in mobility aids, neuro-driven interfaces, and embedded intelligence.

I am eager to connect with labs, professors, startups, and R&D teams working on next-generation robotics or applied AI systems.

Let’s build the future — thoughtfully, accessibly, and together.
